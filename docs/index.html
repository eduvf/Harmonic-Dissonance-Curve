Harmonic Dissonance Curve (beta)

  1. Click load sound
  2. Play the sound
  3. Click 'average' once to start saving the buffer as the sound is playing
  4. Click 'average' again to stop recording the buffer
  5. Use 'exaggerate', the slider & 'smooth' to manipulate the shape
  6. Click 'keep' once you're done
  7. Click 'dissonance' to draw the harmonic dissonance curve
